About TechnoBlog

Explore the wonders that lies within the world of technology. TechnoBlog walks you through the field of technology, and brings you the latest happenings on the market.







Nowadays, technology continues to become a bigger part of everyone's lives - from simple things such as alarms and listening to music, to a bigger scale like education, communication and transportation. The biggest part of our population spend its time mostly immersed in gadgets and machines.

However, most of those who engage in technology are unaware of the dangers of its misuse, and at the same time its potential to do more than what they know. In response to this, we, as Computer Science students, created this website to share information and educate everyone about the use and importance of technology.
